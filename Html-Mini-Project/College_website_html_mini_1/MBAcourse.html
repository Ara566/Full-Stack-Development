What is MBA Course ?

The Master of Business Administration, better known as MBA, is a prestigious postgraduate degree course that offers intensive training in business and management practices.
Its roots go back to 1908, when Harvard University Graduate School of Administration, now recognized as Harvard Business School, launched it as the pioneering postgraduate degree of its kind on a global scale.
The MBA program is meticulously designed to cultivate essential leadership skills, such as robust analytical acumen, sophisticated critical thinking abilities, and sound decision-making prowess.
Furthermore, an MBA enhances communication proficiency and collaborative capabilities, fostering efficient teamwork. Notably, the knowledge and skills garnered from an MBA prepare students for thriving in dynamic leadership roles across various industries worldwide.

The Syllabus for the Master of Business Administration can be divided into 4 semesters. The MBA Syllabus is curated according to industry standards and helps the student get proper placements. The first semester and second semesters deal mostly with theory and core subjects. There is a compulsory internship in the 4th semester.

Type of MBA in BITS

Executive MBA (EMBA)

Executive MBA is one of the best types of MBA for working professionals which is a 1-year course that focuses on business and is specially meant for working professionals with extensive experience. Students pursuing Executive MBA from the top MBA colleges in India get to study management topics like Marketing, Human Resource Management, International Business, and Microfinance. Designed to educate job seekers, managers, entrepreneurs, technocrats, and other business leaders, it relies heavily on teamwork with a striving to achieve a common goal. It should be noted that there is a difference between Executive MBA vs MBA for Executives The course curriculum also introduces them to Strategic Management and Executive Communication.

Executive MBA: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates who wish to study EMBA course must satisfy the following eligibility parameters:


*For admission in this type of MBA Course a minimum of 50% marks at Higher & Senior Secondary level (10th & 12th) Engineering/B.Sc./BBA/B.Com graduates with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized university

*Preference will be given to candidates with a minimum of 2 years of work experience and above for this type of MBA specialisation.
