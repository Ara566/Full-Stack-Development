Admissions

Admissions to regular B.Tech programmes are made based on the rank secured in Engineering, Agriculture & Medicine common entrance test (EAMCET) conducted by Telengana State Council of Higher Education, Warangal.
Three year diploma holders, with engineering common entrance test (ECET) ranking are eligible for admission into B.Tech Programmes.
