Bachelor of technology(B.Tech)

Bachelor of Technology or B Tech is an undergraduate programme in engineering and one of the sought-after courses in India. A degree in Bachelor of Technology is often considered a gateway to the field of engineering. The BTech course duration is four years, depending on the country and the institution. The alternative course to a Bachelor of Technology is Bachelor of Engineering. BE or Bachelor of Engineering is more of a theoretical-based programme while a Bachelor of Technology is based on practical learning as well as theoretical learning.
